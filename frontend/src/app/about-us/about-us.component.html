Evolving with time

In these fast paced, internet driven moments in our civilization’s educational history, the importance of a teacher’s attention and commitment to each pupil is higher than ever before. And that is exactly what we provide. Our teaching methods are always evolving through a carefully planned curriculum, based on the previous results and individual needs of our pupils.

Our classes are limited in size. That allows our teachers to maximise the time spent on each pupil and, in return, to maximise our pupils' capabilities and progress in all areas of their social, emotional and academic development. Also, the increased attention on each pupil develops their critical thinking much faster and, in later stages of their development, our pupils will have an advanced ability to not only think critically, but to think rationally as well.

Education is the soul of a society

Education connects you with the stories and accomplishments of faraway cultures and generations. It offers you a body of knowledge greater than you can ever gain on your own in one lifetime. That is why we, as an educational institution, take great pride in the fact that our school has pupils from more than 20 different nationalities. Bringing people together and breaking barriers between them is what education is all about.
